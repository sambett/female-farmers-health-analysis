# Health Analysis Summary Report

Date: 2025-04-01
Dataset: Female Farmers Health Study
Sample Size: 80

## Key Findings

No significant health relationships were identified. This might be due to the small sample size or data quality issues.

## Analysis Methods

The following health analyses were performed:
- Relationships between BMI and health outcomes
- Blood pressure analysis and its determinants
- Impact of traditional practices (Tabouna smoke, Neffa) on health
- Work patterns and health relationships
- Transportation method and health outcomes
- Agricultural experience and health metrics

## Data Limitations

- Small sample size limiting statistical power
- Missing values in some key variables
- Self-reported health complaints may be subject to recall bias
